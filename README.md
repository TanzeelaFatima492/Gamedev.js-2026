## 🎮 About the Game

**Machine Manager** is a real-time survival game where you control a complex industrial machine. Your goal is simple but challenging: **keep both Temperature and Pressure in the safe zone for as long as possible.**

The machine naturally heats up and builds pressure over time. As you survive longer, the machine becomes harder to control. One wrong move and it's game over!

---

## 🎯 How to Play

| Action | Button | Keyboard |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Cool down the machine | ❄️ COOL | **C** |
| Release pressure | 💨 VENT | **V** |
| Restart the game | 🔄 RESTART | **R** |

### Game Rules

- ✅ **Safe Zone:** Temperature & Pressure between 30% - 70%
- ⚠️ **Warning Zone:** 70% - 85% (machine becoming unstable)
- 💀 **Danger Zone:** Above 85% or below 0% (risk of failure)
- 💥 **Game Over:** Either meter reaches 100% or 0%

### Strategy Tips

1. **Don't wait too long** - reacting early is easier than saving a crisis
2. **Balance is key** - cooling reduces temperature, venting reduces pressure
3. **Survival gets harder** - the machine degrades faster over time
4. **Beat your high score** - best time is saved in your browser

## 🎨 NFT Minting (Ethereum Challenge)

When your machine finally breaks down, you can mint it as a unique NFT! Each minted machine includes:

- Unique ID
- Survival time
- Power rating
- Rarity (Common → Rare → Epic → Legendary)
- Timestamp

NFTs are saved to your browser's local storage and can be viewed in the console.

## 📊 Game Mechanics

### Temperature Meter 🌡️
- Increases naturally over time
- Decreases 15% when using COOL
- Critical above 85% - machine risks explosion

### Pressure Meter 💨
- Increases naturally over time
- Decreases 15% when using VENT
- Critical above 85% - machine risks burst

### Difficulty Scaling
- Base degradation: 1-3% per second
- At 60 seconds: 20% faster
- At 120 seconds: 40% faster
- At 180 seconds: 60% faster
